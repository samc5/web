The Different Pinnipeds

Seals

The seal is a blubbery and lovable marine mammal. There are many different and unique types of seals, some of which live just off the coast of New York

Sea Lions

The sea lion is similar to the seal but with some key differences. Sea lions have flaps for ears, and can walk on land unlike seals, which have no ear flaps and flop on land. It should be noted that fur seals are really sea lions despite the name "seal"

Walruses

The walrus is a large marine mammal known for the two tusks that males have. They are also known for their brilliant mustaches, rivaled by no one
